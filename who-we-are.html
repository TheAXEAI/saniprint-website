Who we are

A factory,
not a form.

SaniPrint is a 3D printing factory in Pleven, Bulgaria. We run a farm of Bambu Lab FDM machines with AMS multi-material systems and turn digital files into physical parts — for businesses that need repeatable production and for individuals who need one object made right.

What we care about

  • Practical manufacturing. Parts that work, tolerances that hold (±0.1–0.2 mm), delivery dates that are realistic.
  • Repeatable production. The 400th unit matches the first. Every batch passes our SQC quality check before dispatch.
  • Flexible quantities. One prototype today, a recurring monthly run next quarter — the process scales without changing partners.
  • Product development. We review geometry, wall thickness, and printability before production, and tell you plainly if a design won't print well.
  • Direct communication. You talk to the people who run the machines. No ticket queues.

B2B and B2C

Businesses use us as an outsourced manufacturing arm: recurring runs, white-label products, packaging, and warehouse delivery. Individuals use SaniCreate to configure and order a single object. Same machines, same quality control.

Local production, European delivery

We produce in Pleven and ship across Bulgaria and the EU with tracked couriers. Producing inside Europe means shorter transit, no customs friction, and easier communication in your time zone.
