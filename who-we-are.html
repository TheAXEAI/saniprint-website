Who we are

A factory,
not a form.

SaniPrint is a B2B 3D printing factory in Pleven, Bulgaria. We turn technical files and product concepts into repeatable physical production for brands, retailers, product teams and industrial clients.

What we care about

  • Practical manufacturing. Parts that work, tolerances that hold (±0.1–0.2 mm), delivery dates that are realistic.
  • Repeatable production. The 400th unit matches the first. Every batch passes our SQC quality check before dispatch.
  • Flexible production stages. Start with a prototype or pilot batch, then scale to recurring production without changing manufacturing partners.
  • Product development. We review geometry, wall thickness, and printability before production, and tell you plainly if a design won't print well.
  • Direct communication. You talk to the people who run the machines. No ticket queues.

Built for B2B production

Business clients use SaniPrint as an outsourced manufacturing arm for prototypes, pilot batches, recurring runs, white-label products, jigs, fixtures, packaging and warehouse delivery.

Local production, European delivery

We produce in Pleven and ship across Bulgaria and the EU with tracked couriers. Producing inside Europe means shorter transit, no customs friction, and easier communication in your time zone.

How we work with business clients

  • One accountable workflow from design review to dispatch.
  • Multi-machine capacity for parallel production.
  • Per-batch quality control and documented specifications.
  • Repeat-order planning around launches, stock levels and monthly demand.
