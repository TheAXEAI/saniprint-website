Process

How it works

01

Send a file, sketch, or description

STL, OBJ, 3MF — or just a photo and dimensions. You don't need a finished model to start.

02

We review the design

Geometry, wall thickness, supports, tolerances. We flag problems before anything prints.

03

Select material, size, quantity

PLA or PETG, layer height from 0.05 to 0.3 mm, single or multi-color via AMS.

04

Receive cost and production estimate

A clear number and a realistic date. No hidden fees added later.

05

Confirm the order

You approve the quote. We schedule the job across the farm.

06

Production begins

Large orders run in parallel on multiple machines to keep turnaround short.

07

Quality control

Every batch passes SQC inspection: dimensional accuracy, surface quality, structural integrity.

08

Delivery

Packed and shipped with tracking, across Bulgaria and Europe.
